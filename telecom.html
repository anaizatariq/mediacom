Mediacom CATV

Regional Business Communications Company

Since 2002, Mediacom CATV (Pvt.) Ltd. has grown into one of Pakistan’s largest regional cable television operators, serving more than 500,000 customers across Faisalabad. Built on a fully underground G.652 fiber-optic infrastructure, the network delivers reliable television services while also supporting enterprise-grade connectivity for corporate clients. Backed by experienced Network Operations Center (NOC) and technical teams, Mediacom ensures rapid response times and consistent service quality. The company also provides television advertising solutions, enabling national and multinational brands to effectively reach one of the region’s largest audiences.

McSOL (Pvt.) Ltd.

A Proven Internet Provider and More

McSOL (Pvt.) Ltd. is one of Faisalabad’s leading fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) internet service providers, delivering high-speed internet, digital television, and voice services through a modern fiber-optic network.

Serving over 35,000 FTTH subscribers, the company continues to expand its network with scalable infrastructure designed for gigabit connectivity. Strategic partnerships with Tier-1 operators, including Transworld and CMPak-Zong, provide a resilient backbone, while dedicated engineering and network operations teams ensure dependable service for residential, commercial, and enterprise customers.

Why You Should Choose Us

McSOL offers a premium digital experience with gigabit internet speeds and a wide range of entertainment options. Our FTTH network ensures uninterrupted service for your home or business.

Equipped with the latest GPON technology, our network provides stable, high-bandwidth connectivity suitable for gaming, streaming, and enterprise applications.

With round-the-clock customer support and technical expertise, we ensure that your digital life operates smoothly without any interruptions.
