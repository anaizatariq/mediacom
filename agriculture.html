Sindhu Agriculture Farms

Large-Scale Agricultural Estate

Sindhu Agriculture & Livestock Farms is a large-scale agricultural estate strategically located near the Sahianwala Interchange on the Faisalabad–Islamabad Motorway along the CPEC corridor, in close proximity to the Faisalabad Industrial Estate Development & Management Company (FIEDMC) and Allama Iqbal Industrial City SEZ. The operation focuses on sustainable farming practices, efficient irrigation systems, and commercial-scale crop production to maximize productivity while preserving long-term soil health. The estate cultivates wheat, sugarcane, and guava orchards, while producing a range of agricultural products that reflect the Group’s commitment to innovation, food security, and responsible land stewardship.

Lyallpur Dairy Farm

State of the Art Dairy Operations

A premium facility dedicated to providing the highest quality milk and meat to consumers through uncompromising standards and exceptional animal care.

Our Herd Composition

Our dairy herd is meticulously managed and categorized into four primary groups to ensure optimal health, animal welfare, and milk production.

Cows The females who give birth to calves and produce the milk.
Bulls The fathers of the dairy herd, carefully selected for genetics.
Heifers Young female cattle maturing into milking cows.
Calves Baby cattle raised in dedicated, nurturing environments.

Our Vision

We have a vision to provide the best environment for our herd and maintain a non-compromising attitude toward delivering fresh, pure products with consistent quality to our customers and peers alike.

Premium Milking

Proper milking procedures, attention to detail, and a pristine environment are strictly maintained to maximize production. Our operations are handled by highly trained professionals who understand they are harvesting premium food.

Friesian Sahiwal

A combination of the highly resilient Pakistani Sahiwal and Holstein breeds, specifically designed for tropical regions. Our cows produce exceptional yields while maintaining high resistance to heat and humidity.
