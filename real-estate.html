Flagship Project

Mediacom Trade City

Developed in 2004, Mediacom Trade City is a flagship 350,000 sq.ft. multi-storey commercial complex situated in the heart of Kohinoor Town, Faisalabad. The modern facility integrates a spacious shopping mall featuring national and multinational retail brands, dedicated food courts, and an expansive underground valet parking system. It also houses executive corporate offices, business centers, and luxury apartments. Supported by uninterrupted power backups, robust security systems, and high-speed elevators, Trade City sets the benchmark for mixed-use commercial real estate in the region.

Shopping Mall

Multi-Story Shopping Mall with dedicated Food Court having multi-national and national brand's outlets like Generation, Maria. B, Cross Stitch. Shopping Mall occupies 2 Floors — Basement and Ground Floor.

Executive Offices

State of the art offices starting from minimum 200 sq. ft with affordable plans on 2 dedicated floors. Your office should reflect the identity of your company.
