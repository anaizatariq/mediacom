About Us

Since 1995, Providing Services in Several Areas of Public Interest

Mediacom Group is a privately held diversified business conglomerate with a legacy spanning over five decades and a strong footprint across Pakistan. Over the years, the Group has evolved into a multi-sector powerhouse with solid financial strength, a robust asset base, and a proven track record of executing and scaling high-value projects.

Our strategic leadership, investment capability and forward‑looking approach enable us to build and operate sustainable ventures in Communications, Media, Technology, Real Estate, Hospitality, Petroleum, Agriculture and Livestock. We pride ourselves on regional strength in Fiber & Media infrastructure, large commercial real estate and emerging technologies, positioning Mediacom among the most influential business groups in the Faisalabad region.
