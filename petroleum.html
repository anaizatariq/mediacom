About Mediacom Petroleum

A reliable stop for retail and commercial energy needs.

Mediacom Petroleum operates a modern fuel retail facility strategically positioned near the Faisalabad Industrial Estate Development & Management Company (FIEDMC), one of Pakistan's largest industrial zones.

Serving both industrial and retail customers, the station provides reliable fuel solutions for commuters, logistics operators, and commercial businesses. Its prime location and commitment to quality service make it an important component of the Group's diversified portfolio.
