CEO & Founder

Nadeem Aftab Sindhu

Nadeem Aftab Sindhu is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Mediacom Group, a diversified business conglomerate with interests spanning telecommunications, technology, commercial real estate, agriculture, livestock, and strategic investments. For over three decades, he has led the Group’s growth through disciplined entrepreneurship, long-term vision, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Under his leadership, Mediacom has established one of the region’s most advanced fibre-optic communication networks while successfully developing landmark commercial and residential projects and expanding into modern agriculture and livestock ventures. His focus on innovation, operational excellence, and sustainable growth continues to shape the Group’s evolution across multiple industries.

Beyond business, Mr. Sindhu has served in several prominent public leadership roles, including Town Nazim (Mayor) of Lyallpur Town, Chairman of FESCO, and positions on various public and industry boards, reflecting his longstanding commitment to economic development and public service.
