McSOL (Pvt.) Ltd.

Fast, Reliable Fiber-to-the-Home

McSOL (Pvt.) Ltd. is one of Faisalabad's leading fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) internet service providers, delivering high-speed internet, digital television, and voice services through a modern fiber-optic network. Serving over 35,000 FTTH subscribers, the company continues to expand its network with scalable infrastructure designed for gigabit connectivity.

Strategic partnerships with Tier-1 operators, including Transworld and CMPak‑Zong, provide a resilient backbone, while dedicated engineering and network operations teams ensure dependable service for residential, commercial, and enterprise customers.
